Chile

Geographic location

Located in the continent of South America, Chile covers 743,812 square kilometers of land and 12,290 square kilometers of water, making it the 38th largest nation in the world with a total area of 756,102 square kilometers.

Language

Spanish is the most widely spoken language in Chile. About 14 million Chileans speak the language. Several other indigenous and immigrant languages are also spoken in the country.

Climate

In Chile, there is a wide variety of climates: desert but mild in the north, Mediterranean in the center, oceanic in the south, and cold in the Andes. Being located in the Southern Hemisphere, in Chile the seasons are reversed in comparison with North America or Europe.
